Our Story

Nicole has been singing in the St. Ignatius Lifeteen band since 2010. Nick joined the band as the pianist in 2018, but a year and a half passed before they had a conversation that lasted longer than “hey... hi…” If you know Nick, then you'll understand why!

On the Feast of Corpus Christi, Nicole sang the refrain of “Amazing Love” over 20 times while processing around the church. That night, Nick heard Nicole's voice in his dreams. It continued after he awoke, and he found that he could not escape from the sound. This compelled him to finally ask her on a date.

That first date led to many other dates, and after 2 years of dates, they decided they should probably just go on dates with each other for the rest of their lives. Nick made it official when he proposed to Nicole in front of Anna Ruby Falls, in Helen, Georgia. You're reading this because Nicole obviously said "yes"!
